Hamster Keeper

A productivity tool focused on logging items with your camera and providing expiry reminders. Just snap a pic to log it! It's simple, convenient, and incredibly useful!

✨ Key Features

  • 📷 Intelligent Photo Entry: Recognize items and dates from photos to create records automatically.
  • 📅 Expiry Reminders: Get notified for your items, neatly grouped into 'Remaining', 'Expiring Soon', and 'Expired'.
  • 📝 Drafts Box: After recognition, fine-tune the details in a draft before adding it to your main list.
  • 🔒 Cloud Sync: Sign in to keep your data synchronized across all your devices.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Camera Won't Open

Please check if you have granted camera and microphone permissions:
Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera → Hamster Keeper

App Crashes or Freezes

• Restart the App
• Restart your device
• Make sure your iOS version is 14.0 or later
• Free up some storage space on your device
